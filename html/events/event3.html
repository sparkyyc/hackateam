AnalyticsX

The AnalyticsX hackathon will be held in Fort Collins Colorado for the first time. AnalyticsX is excited to meet and network with everyone who goes. For this hackathon AnalyticsX will accept all levels of code experience.

In this hackathon, your objective is to build any API you want, to then access it and display it on a webpage. You must style the webpage to then be hand picked by 6 judges. The website that looks the best and has the most functionality wil be the winner.

Details:

  • The hackathon is from 11am-5pm.
  • All groups must be checked in by 11am, to then start at 11:15am.
  • You will have five hours to complete your project.
  • You will need to bring your own computer and charger.
  • You will need to bring your own lunch, but beverages will be provided.
  • After presentations and anouncing the winners, there will be a cake party from 5pm-5:30pm.

Event Date: 1/6/2019

Address: 2473 Drake Ave, Fort Collins CO, 80521
