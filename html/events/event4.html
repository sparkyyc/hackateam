Reloaded

The Reloaded hackathon will be held in Colorado Springs for the ninth time. Team Reloaded is celebrating their 30th year in the tech industry by hosting a hackathon. They do accept all levels of coding knowledge and will give you a free shirt if you are a part of it.

In this hackathon, you will need to build a web application that lets farmers know if it's a good day to plant or harvest.

Details:

  • The hackathon is from 8am-7:30pm.
  • All groups must be checked in by 9am, to then start at 9:15am.
  • You will have 10 hours to complete your project.
  • You will need to bring your own computer and charger.
  • They will cater food and have beer and other drinks on tap.
  • After presentations they will have a cocktail hour from 6:30pm-7:30pm.

Event Date: 12/18/2018

Address: 2112 Ash Blvd, Colorado Springs CO, 80829

by Amanda Dillon, 10/29/2018
