Unearthed

The Unearthed hackathon will be held in Aurora Colorado for the first time. Starting in Vancouver, Unearthed is proud to invite people from all technical backgrounds to this event.

In this hackathon, you will need to find and build a solution for water pollution caused from mining. This could be finding alternative ways for mining, building an application to show other's where to dump in areas that won't affect rivers or ground water, etc..

Details:

  • All food and drinks will be free
  • The hackathon is from 9am-7:30pm.
  • All groups must be checked in by 10am, to then start at 10:15am.
  • You will have eight hours to complete your project.
  • You will need to bring your own computer and charger.
  • They will cater food and have beer and other drinks on tap.
  • After presentations, and announcing the winners they will have a cocktail hour from 6pm-7pm.

Event Date: 11/15/2018

Address: 3118 Sampson Street, Aurora CO, 80014

by Christa Sparks, 10/29/2018
